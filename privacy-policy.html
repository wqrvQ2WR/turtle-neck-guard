Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

Turtle Neck Guard ("the Extension") is a Chrome extension that detects forward head posture in real time using your device's webcam.

Data Collection

The Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Camera footage is processed entirely on your local device and is never sent to any external server.

Camera Usage

The webcam is accessed solely to analyze your neck posture in real time. No images or video are recorded, saved, or shared.

Third-Party Services

The Extension loads an AI posture model from teachablemachine.withgoogle.com (Google). Only model weight files are downloaded — no user data is sent to this service.

Contact

If you have any questions, please contact: wqrvQ2WR via GitHub.
